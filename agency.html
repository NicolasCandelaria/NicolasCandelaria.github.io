FOUNDER & PRINCIPAL · SINCE DAY ONE, END TO END

Running a studio is product judgment with real stakes.

Every engagement at OnePath runs through me end to end: scoping, estimates, timelines, delivery, and the client relationship. Eighty-plus projects means eighty-plus rounds of deciding what to build, what to cut, and what to say no to, with my own name on the outcome.

L.01

Scope is a design material

The best deliverable is often smaller than the one requested. Learning to shape scope, not just fill it, changed how I design everything.

L.02

Estimates are promises

When your own name is on the invoice, a timeline is a commitment, not a guess. That discipline followed me into every agency project since.

L.03

Parting with my co-founder

OnePath began as two names. When our directions quietly stopped agreeing, I ended the partnership and carried the studio alone. It cost a friendship and bought the work its clarity: one voice, one standard, one person answering for every promise that leaves this studio.
